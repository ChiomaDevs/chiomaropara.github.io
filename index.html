About Me

My story

I'm Chioma Opara — a Digital Transformation Leader with 8+ years of experience driving innovation across banking, fintech, and smart infrastructure.

My career began in retail banking at Guaranty Trust Bank, where I managed 1,000+ client accounts and turned a struggling team's balance sheet positive within three months. I moved into product marketing and owned the P&L for GTCollections, the bank's bill payment platform — growing annual digital payment revenue by 121% (from ₦333M to ₦737M) and launching integrations that generated ₦416M in additional revenue.

From banking, I transitioned into quality assurance, implementing automated testing that cut post-release defects by 30%. Then I took on my current role at Lagos Free Zone (Tolaram), where I lead technology initiatives for Nigeria's first smart free zone — automating key manual workflows, delivering a 10-module Sustainability Portal for real-time ESG tracking, and managing a $250K vendor portfolio.

Along the way, I founded Moms in Project Management (MiPM), a nonprofit equipping mothers to transition into project-management careers. I'm also passionate about AI adoption — I've designed and delivered workshops for 200+ employees, boosting workplace AI-tool usage from 10% to 40% in just 3 months.
