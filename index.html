About Me

The short version

I'm Chioma Opara — an IT Project Manager and Digital Transformation Consultant with over a decade of experience spanning fintech, free trade zones, and enterprise operations.

Most recently, I've been leading the IT function at Lagos Free Zone (Tolaram), where I drove the shift from paper-based processes to fully digital, automated systems. I've directed the delivery of a 10-module sustainability portal, led cross-functional teams of 20+ people, and partnered across departments to turn operational bottlenecks into streamlined workflows.

Before that, I spent several years in banking — at Guaranty Trust Bank — building products, managing channels, and ensuring quality across technology delivery.

I hold an MBA from Nexford University, a Master's in IT from the University of Lagos, and carry PMP® and PSPO certifications. I bring the rare combination of strategic thinking and hands-on execution that complex projects demand.
